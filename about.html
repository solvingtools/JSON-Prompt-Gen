About Us

Our Mission

JSON Prompt Gen exists to bridge the gap between creative vision and technical execution. As AI video models like Google Veo, OpenAI Sora, and Runway Gen-3 become more capable, the complexity of controlling them increases.

We believe filmmakers should focus on shots, lighting, and angles—not JSON syntax errors.

The Development Philosophy

We built this tool with a strict "Local-First" methodology. In an era where user data is constantly mined, we took a radical step back:

  • No Servers: We don't want your data. We don't need your data.
  • No Accounts: Creativity shouldn't require a login.
  • Open Code: The prompt structures we generate are transparent and editable.

The Developer

JSON Prompt Gen is the passion project of a dedicated solo developer obsessed with the intersection of cinema and code.

Recognizing that "prompt engineering" is just modern cinematography, I built this tool to help creators translate their visual language into the rigorous structured data that next-gen AI models demand.

Every feature—from the "Golden Hour" lighting preset to the camera movement logic—is crafted to save you time and maximize your creative output.
